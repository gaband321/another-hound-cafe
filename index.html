Our Story

A journey of flavors,
crafted for discovery.

Another Hound Café is where classical Western comfort food meets the bold, complex flavours of Asia. We source the finest ingredients — Royal Project highland produce, sustainably grown herbs, and carefully selected proteins — then transform them into dishes that feel familiar yet surprising.

Casual. Stylish. Innovative. Find us across three of Bangkok's most iconic addresses.
